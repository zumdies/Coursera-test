Audiobooks

Life Without Ed

“Life Without Ed” by Jenni Schaefer is an easy to read and a very practical book based on her own experiences recovering from an eating disorder. Jenni has written the book in conjunction with her psychotherapist, Thom Rutledge, who gives insightful exercises at the end of each section for interested readers who may be recovering or working with clients with an eating disorder.

Banker to the World

Banker To The World is firstly a career memoir by a man in the middle of everything from 1957 to the new millennium, and it’s also a “how to” book of sorts with each chapter offering not just banking lessons, but also broad lessons on how to thrive in any field.

Success

Napoleon Hill's landmark Think and Grow Rich remains one of the biggest bestsellers of all time, with over 20 million copies in print and translated into more than 30 languages. Hill's philosophy of personal achievement, wealth, and empowerment created millionaires the world over.
